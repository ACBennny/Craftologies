Sign Up

This section is for new or old customers who wish to create an account
If you already have an account kindly log in.

Name

This is your unique identif that would be used to identify you on our platform in regards to services being rendered. Example; online orders, payments, etcetera.
Kindly note the following when creating your username

Your name given should be your legal name as we document every purchase/services rendered

Username can NOT contain special characters ; which includes but is not limited to:
'' "" @ # $ % ^ ` & * () = ; , <> . {} [] \ ! | ~ / + - ?

Password

A password is required in order to secure your account. It should have the following format:

Password must contain at least one of 'a-z', 'A-Z', '0-9', special characters eg; @ # $ % ^ ` & * () = ; , <> . {} [] \ ! | ~ / + - ?

Password must be a minimum length of "8" characters

Password must be the same for confirm password for it to be accepted

Email

A valid working email is required for logging in and recovering your account if necessary.
Please note that It will be used for verification before you can create your account.
